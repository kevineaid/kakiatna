MESSAGE FROM OUR CEO

Bismillahirrahmanirrahim,

As we seek to improve the quality of people living in Indonesia and also increase the size of industrialization, electricity remains the most underlying element in achieving these goals. Yet, the growth in this area is kept at a limited rate for a sustained period. With our resource and experience, I believe we could be one of the best-positioned power producers to spur the progress.

In addition, responding to the country's middle-class population hike and, not least, to fulfill our internal roadmap, in recent years we have launched many other businesses spanning in several key areas, including engineering, digital media, retail and film production & distribution. I believe this type of diversification will strengthen our position, and exciting opportunities have presented themselves in the aforementioned areas.

An important task for our company is to maintain the belief that our partners have put in us and to implement strategies that we have to enable strong growth and profit. Indonesia is just beginning to open itself to the rest of the world, and what we've seen today is just tip of the iceberg. In decades to come, the possibilities are endless. I am confident of the team's ability to manage through the increasingly challenging and exciting era for the business climate, and to meet the expectations of our stakeholders. We are set on the belief that Indonesia is going to be a major player in world economy. Mari berkarya untuk Indonesia!

Yours Sincerely,

Willawati

History

Kakiatna Indonesia is the holding company of Kakiatna Group, a conglomerate of companies whose range of operations span in various industries from Energy, Food & Beverages, Retail, Film Production & Distirbution, to Engineering and Construction. Kakiatna Indonesia was founded in 2009 under a different name of Gizania Group. In 2014, we decided to change our name into Kakiatna Indonesia, meaning strong and solid in Sundanese. As the name implies, we are committing ourselves for a change in Indonesia through a strong and solid business foundation.
